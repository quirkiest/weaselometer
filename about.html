About

The WeaselOMeter is here to fight back against the rising tide of AI slop, consultant-driven content and the general dross in which prose is increasingly drenched.

Our clever little weasel works inside your browser to detect multiple kinds of guff:

AI Slop — no, we are not a sophisticated “AI or not” detector, but we're here to spare you from the most notorious phrases that our ubiquitous digital assistants seem to love to foist upon us.
Consultant Bingo — words created by the Best and Brightest of that superior class of people who are paid by the syllable.
Weaselwords — the linguistic Nothing Burger: words and phrases that seem to say something but neatly avoid being measurable or taking responsibility for anything except wasting our collective time.
(E)TLAs — acronyms are helpful, but not if your writing looks like your Caps Lock key is high on cocaine.

The WeaselOMeter is a community service brought to you by the data geeks at Data-Driven Australia.
