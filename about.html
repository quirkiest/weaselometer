About

The WeaselOMeter is here to fight back against the rising tide of AI slop, consultant-driven content and the general dross in which prose is increasingly drenched.

Our clever little weasel works inside your browser to detect multiple kinds of guff:

AI Slop
No, we are not a sophisticated “AI or not” detector, but we're here to spare you from the most notorious phrases that our ubiquitous digital assistants seem to love to foist upon us.
Consultant Bingo
Words created by the Best and Brightest of that superior class of people who are paid by the syllable.
Weaselwords
The linguistic Nothing Burger: words and phrases that seem to say something but neatly avoid being measurable or taking responsibility for anything except wasting our collective time.
(E)TLAs
Acronyms are helpful, but not if your writing looks like your Caps Lock key is high on cocaine.
